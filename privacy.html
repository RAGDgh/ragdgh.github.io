Privacy Policy

At Cherry Picker APP, we provide professional rideshare drivers with powerful tools while maintaining strict data privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection & Licensing

To provide secure license management, we collect a unique Hardware Identifier (HWID) from your Android device. This identifier is used exclusively to activate your subscription and prevent unauthorized use of your license key.

2. What We DO NOT Collect

We believe your business data is yours alone. Cherry Picker APP does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your real-time GPS location history.
  • Your passenger or trip details.
  • Your personal contact information or phone logs.

3. Payment Security

All financial transactions are handled securely by Lemon Squeezy. Cherry Picker APP never sees or stores your credit card numbers or bank details.
