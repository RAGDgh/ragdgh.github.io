Cherry Picker is strictly a decision-support utility. To ensure your account safety and privacy:

  • No Automation: Cherry Picker never interacts with your phone's OS to accept, decline, or cancel trips. You retain 100% manual control.

You are the pilot. Cherry Picker provides the data, but you make the decisions. We are not responsible for your acceptance rates, account standing with rideshare platforms, or traffic safety. Do not interact with the app while the vehicle is in motion.

2. License & Device Binding

Your license is tied to your specific Android device via Hardware ID (HWID). This is used strictly for subscription verification. One license equals one active device.

3. Subscription & Trial

Users receive a 3-day free trial. After the trial, the subscription auto-renews monthly via Lemon Squeezy. You can cancel at any time to stop future billing.

Cherry Picker provides mathematical estimates. While we aim for 100% accuracy, final payouts are determined by your rideshare platform. We do not guarantee specific income levels.
